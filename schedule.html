Schedule to Relive Our School Days

On 22nd December 2024, we're taking a trip down memory lane with a schedule that brings back the joy of our school days at LFC! The day will begin with a warm welcome at the campus gates, followed by a special assembly that recreates those moments of gathering together in the school courtyard, just like old times. We'll have the chance to participate in nostalgic classroom sessions and revisit our favorite spots on campus. There will be time to share stories, enjoy lunch together in the canteen, and participate in some lighthearted games and activities in the playground. As the day winds down, an evening program filled with music, memories, and celebrations awaits, all bringing us back to the spirit of unity and friendship that made our school years so unforgettable.
